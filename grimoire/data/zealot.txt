## Cleric Spells

#Unhallow (reversible)

Spiritual Armor
Level: 1
Duration: Combat
Range: Caster
The caster is surrounded with a divine glow which repulses blows.
Providing +2 AC bonus and may be "sacrificed" as a shield.
[From Barrowmaze by Greg Gillespie]

Cure Blindness
Level: 2
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Cures magical or physical damage to any one sense; sight, hearing, taste, touch,
or smell.

Cure Disease
Level: 3
Duration: Permanent
Range: 30'
Instantly cures all diseases in range. Also, destroys all green slime, moulds
and similar yucky things.

Cause Disease
Level: 3
Duration: Permanent
Range: 30'
Inflicts a nasty disease on one creature, no save. Victim is -2 to all
actions, can't regain hit points or be cured of damage magically or naturally
until disease is removed.

Remove Curse
Level: 4
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Instantaneously removes one curse on a creature including allowing it to
remove cursed item.

Curse
Level: 4
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Curse can bring about any number of unfortunate effects, determined by the
caster and Referee. There is typically no save. Can be used to curse objects
which then affect anyone who touches the object.

Heal
Level: 4
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Channels divine energy into a creature healing all CON damage and immediately
ends any and all health related adverse conditions such as: blindness,
disease, fatigue, feeblemind, insanity, and poison.  It also allows Hit Dice
re-roll.

Harm
Level: 4
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Channels profane energy into creature taking away all hit points, all but d4
points of CON, inflicting blindness, deafness, and the same disease as |Cause
Disease|_. If also forces D&D roll at -2. Save vs each affliction to
individually negate.

Delay Poison
Level: 1
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: Touch
The effect of any poison to which target is exposed is delayed until spell's
duration has expired.  If used soon after death, will restore to life until
the spell duration ends a victim of poison.

Neutralize Poison
Level: 3
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Detoxifies any sort of venom or poison in the creature or object touched.
If used soon after death will bring back to life a victim of poison.

Poison
Level: 3
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Save or die.  Alternatively, may be used to poison food, drink, or a weapon.

Aerial Servant
Level: 4
Duration: 1 day per level
Range: Special
Summons an Aerial Servant which is forced to perform a service other than
combat.

Animate Dead
Level: 3
Duration: Permanent until dispelled
Range: 60'
Caster may animate a number of HD equal to twice their level.

Animate Objects
Level: 3
Duration: 1 hour per level
Range: 60'
Imbues inanimate objects with mobility and a semblance of life which attacks
whomever or whatever the caster designates.  This spell cannot animate objects
carried or worn by a creature.

Spiritual Weapon
Level: 1
Duration: Combat
Range: 30'
Conjures an animated weapon of pure force which independently attacks
opponents dealing d6 damage (+1 damage die size per three caster levels).

Striking
Level: 2
Duration: Combat
Range: Touch
Enchants one weapon to deal an extra d6 of damage and be able to damage
creatures only affected by magical weapons.

Enchant Arms
Level: 3
Duration: Combat
Range: 30'
Enchants all allied weapons to deal and extra d6 of damage and be able to
damage creatures only affected by magical weapons.

Call Lightning
Level: 1
Duration: 10 rounds per level
Range: Sight
The caster must be outdoors and in a stormy area; a rain shower, clouds and
wind, hot and cloudy conditions, or even a tornado.  A vertical bolt of
lightning may be called once every 10 rounds. Dealing 2d8, +1d8 per level
points of electricity damage, save for 1/2, to all within a 10' radius or in
the path of the bolt.

Blade Barrier
Level: 3
Duration: 3 rounds per level
Range: 60'
Conjures an immobile, vertical curtain of whirling blades. Either in a circle
10' radius or a wall up to 200 square feet. Any creature passing
through the wall takes 6d10 points of damage.

Flame Strike
Level: 4
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 240'
Evokes a vertical column of divine retribution 30' high and 10' in diameter that
roars downward dealing 6d8 hit points of holy fire damage, no save for target,
others save for 1/2.

Bless
Level: 1
Duration: 6 turns
Range: Touch
Bless fills one ally with courage and divine purpose. They are bestowed a +2
morale bonus and +2 to hit.  Casting on a weapon will increase its damage
die by 1 size (by 2 vs "evil" creatures).  Cannot be cast during combat.

Chant
Level: 1
Duration: Concentration
Range: Sound
Intoned continuously, brings about supernatural intervention on behalf of the
caster and allies within range. All attacks, and saving throws are made
with a +2 bonus. Likewise, all of these rolls attempted by enemies suffer a -2
penalty. This effect continues so long as the caster does not move and focuses
only on chanting. However, if they are dealt damage, or otherwise physically
distracted, the spell ends.

Prayer
Level: 2
Duration: Combat
Range: 30'
Similar to |Chant|_ bestows divine aide to the caster and allies. All attacks,
and saving throws are made with a +2 bonus. Likewise, all of these rolls
attempted by enemies suffer a -2 penalty.

Command
Level: 1
Duration: 1 round
Range: Sound
Caster gives one target a single word command, which it obeys to the best of
its ability.  Targets with 5+ HD, or have 12+ INT may save to negate.  The
single word must make sense as a command, such as approach, drop, fall, flee,
halt, surrender, sleep, etc. Although a target could be instructed to "die,"
this will only make the target take on a comatose state for a single round.
The target must be able to understand the language of the caster.

Divine Weather
Level: 1
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 0
Divines weather conditions 2 hours per caster level into the future.

Augury
Level: 1
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 0
Portends whether a particular action will bring good or bad results to caster
and allies in the immediate future.

Divination
Level: 3
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 0
Provides caster with useful information regarding an area, building, section of
a dungeon, and other similar places.

Commune
Level: 4
Duration: 3 turns
Range: 0
Calls upon higher powers to answer up to three "yes" or "no" questions.
Higher powers do not look kindly upon those who call upon them for trivial
matters, or who call upon them too often.

Conjure Animals
Level: 1
Duration: 1 combat
Range: 60'
Conjures 1 HD per caster level of normal animals out of thin air to attack
enemies designated by the caster. Animals will fight to the death or until
the end of combat and then disappear.

Light (reversible)
Level: 1
Duration: 6 turns
Range: 120'
Creates light as bright as a torch in 20' radius. Can be cast on eyes,
blinding target, save negates.

Darkness
Level: 1
Duration: 6 turns
Range: 120'
Creates a 15' radius sphere of darkness. This darkness is complete, blocking
normal and supernatural vision.  Can be cast on eyes, blinding target, save
negates.

Continual Light
Level: 2
Duration: 1 hour per level
Range: 120'
Produces light as bright as sunlight in a 30' radius around target which may
be an object or point in space. "Darkness" spells of less than 3rd level
cannot diminish this light. Any creatures that suffer penalties (not including
damage) for being in sunlight are affected by this spell. Can also be cast on
a creature's eyes, causing blindness, save negates.

Continual Darkness
Level: 2
Duration: 1 hour per level
Range: 120'
Produces profound darkness in a 30' radius around target which may be an
object or point in space. Normal eyesight, including infravision, cannot
penetrate this darkness, nor can lamps, torches, or spells of less than 3rd
level.  Can be cast on a creature's eyes, causing blindness, save negates.

Control Temperature
Level: 2
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: 0
Within 30' radius caster may raise or lower the ambient temperature by 10
degrees (Fahrenheit) per caster level.

Control Winds
Level: 3
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: 0
Alter wind force within a 30' radius per caster level. For every caster level,
wind can increase or decrease speed by 10 miles per hour, in increments of 10
miles per hour per round.  A 10' radius "eye" of calm air optionally surrounds
the caster.
**Strong winds (30+ mph):** make sailing difficult, missile
weapons difficult, and forces smaller airborne creatures
out of the air.
**Severe wind (45+ mph):** causes minor ship and
building damage, and forces human-sized airborne
creatures from the air.
**Windstorm (60+ mph):** drives most flying creatures
from the skies, uproots small trees, knocks down light
wooden structures, tears off roofs, and endangers
ships.
**Hurricane force winds (75+ mph):** destroy wooden
buildings, sometimes uproot even large trees, and
cause most ships to sink.

Control Weather
Level: 4
Duration: 4d12 hours
Range: several miles
The caster may call forth weather appropriate to the climate and season of the
area.  Control weather can do away with atmospheric phenomena (naturally
occurring or otherwise) as well as create them.  The caster controls the
general tendencies of the weather, such as the direction and intensity of the
wind, but cannot control specific applications of the weather; where lightning
strikes, or the exact path of a tornado.
It takes 1 turn to cast the spell and an additional 1d4 turns for the
effects to manifest.

Control Weather, Greater
Level: 5
Duration: 4d12 hours
Range: many miles
As |Control Weather|_ but may change weather to be consistent
with an adjacent season.

Create Food and Water
Level: 3
Duration: Permanent
Range: 10'
Conjures enough food and water to sustain 3 hungry people per level for a day.

Detect Evil
Level: 1
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: 10' per level
The caster percieves "Evil" creatures and objects within range to glow evilly.

Find Traps
Level: 1
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: Sight
The caster can perceive both magical and non-magical traps.  This spell grants
no knowledge about the nature of the trap or how to deactivate it.

Earthquake
Level: 7
Duration: d6 rounds
Range: 240'
Causes an intense but highly localized tremor 10'r per caster level, during
which creatures on the ground cannot move, cast spells or attack.
**Cave, Cavern, or Tunnel**: collapses, dealing 7d6 points of damage.  **Cliffs,
Mountains**: landslides, dealing 7d6 points of damage.  **Open
Ground**: fissures open, creatures must save or fall into one and die.  **Structure**:
collapses typical wooden or masonry buildings, but not those of
stone or reinforced, dealing 7d6 points of damage.  **River, Lake, or Marsh**:
fissures open underneath the water, draining it away and forming muddy ground,
creatures may be sucked under and killed.

Exorcise
Level: 3
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Removes foreign influences from target, whether those influences are from
|Magic Jar|_, charms, possession, and similar effects.

Find the Path (reversible)
Level: 3
Duration: 1 turn per level, 1 day outdoors
Range: Touch
Target can find the shortest, most direct route to a specified destination,
whether into or out of a locale. The locale can be outdoors, underground, or
even inside a maze spell. Find the path works with respect to locations, not
objects or creatures, indicating, at appropriate times, the exact path to
follow or physical actions to take.  Find the Path can be used to remove the
caster and companions from the effect of a |Maze|_ spell in a single round.

Gate
Level: 7
Duration: see below
Range: 30'
The spell functions exactly as the magic-user spell of the same
name.

Glyph of Warding
Level: 3
Duration: Until triggered
Range: Touch
This powerful inscription harms those who enter, pass, or open the warded area
or object. A glyph can ward a bridge or passage, portal, chest, box, room, and
so on. The area of effect is up to 5' squared per caster level.  A glyph can
be placed to conform to any shape up to the limitations of the total square
footage. When the spell is completed, the glyph and tracery become nearly
invisible.  Any creature entering or touching the warded area or opening the
warded object without speaking a password triggers the glyph.

**Blast Glyph**: Deals 2 points of elemental damage per caster level, save for 1/2.

**Spell Glyph**: Casts one spell such as |Amnesia|_ or |Hold Person|_.

Hold Person
Level: 2
Duration: 9 turns
Range: 120'
Paralyzes 1-4 humanoids of caster's choosing, save negates.  If less than
4 creatures are targeted, each saves at -1 per creature fewer.  Undead,
mindless and ogre sized or larger targets are unaffected. Targets are aware
and breathe normally but cannot take any actions, even speech.

Dispel Evil
Level: 4
Duration: Concentration
Range: 30'
Caster can take no other action but concentrate on the spell for the entire
duration. All "evil" and conjured creatures that come within 30' of the caster
must save or be destroyed.  Any creature that succeeds will flee.  It also
functions as a |Dispel Magic|_ vs all "evil" spells and items within range.
Alternatively, the caster can direct the spell at one target only which must
save at -4.

Holy Word
Level: 4
Duration: Special
Range: 60'
Upon speaking the holy word, any oppositely aligned creature within a 60' area
of the caster suffers the following ill effects, no save.
======= ==================================
HD      Effect
======= ==================================
12+     Deafened, -2 to hit for 1d6 rounds
8-11    Stunned for 2d6 rounds
4-7     Paralyzed for 1d6 turns
<=3     Killed
======= ==================================

Insect Plague
Level: 5
Duration: 1 day
Range: 480'
Summons a 60' swarm of flying, biting insects under the caster's control.  It
moves at running speed. Any creatures engulfed by the swarm suffer d4 points
of damage per round due to the numerous bites and stings.  Creatures of 2 HD or
fewer will flee in terror from the insect plague, no save.

Locate Object
Level: 2
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: 20' per level
Caster senses the direction (as the crow flies) towards a well-known or
clearly visualized object named when casting the spell. Will locate nearest
example of generic objects, e.g. "stairs".  Attempting to find a specific item
requires a detailed and accurate mental image; if the image is not close
enough to the actual object.

Lower Water
Level: 2
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: 10' per level
Lowers water or other fluid by 10% per caster level.

Part Water
Level: 3
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: 120'
Same as Magic-user spell of same name.

Plane Shift
Level: 5
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: Touch
Moves target to another plane of existence or alternate dimension chosen by
caster, save negates. Up to seven willing targets linked by hands.  Transports
creatures instantaneously and then ends, no return ticket is provided.

Protection
Level: 1
Duration: 12 turns
Range: Touch
Wards one target per level against "evil". Providing +2 bonus to saves from
evil sources. Prevents bodily contact by evil, summoned or conjured creatures.
The protection against contact ends if target takes offensive action
against the warded creature.  Mage version is self only.

Protection from Fear
Level: 1
Duration: 12 turns
Range: Touch
Instills courage in one target per level, granting a +4 save bonus vs fear.
And potentially removing the effects of existing fear by allowing targets
a (new) save.  When warding caster it also grants complete immunity against
the first exposure to fear.  Mage version is self only.

Protection from Enchantment
Level: 2
Duration: 12 turns
Range: Touch
Grants one target per level +4 save bonus vs mind affecting spells that charm,
command, delude, confuse, etc.  Targets currently under such an effect
get a new save with bonus to break free.  When warding caster it also
grants complete immunity against the first exposure to enchantment.  Mage
version is self only.

Resist Element
Level: 1
Duration: 6 turns
Range: Touch
One target per level is unharmed by intense (non-magical) heat. Grants +4 bonus to
all saves vs heat-based attacks.
At time of casting caster may choose another type of elemental damage in place
of fire, such as cold, electricity, or acid.  Mage version is self only.

Protection from Element
Level: 2
Duration: 12 turns
Range: Touch
Grants one target per level complete immunity to normal fire, +4 to saves vs and
cuts damage from magical fire in half. When warding caster it also grants
complete immunity against the first exposure to magical fire.
At time of casting caster may choose another type of elemental damage in place
of fire, such as cold, electricity, or acid.  Mage version is self only.

Life Energy Protection
Level: 1
Duration: 12 turns
Range: Touch
Wards one target per level against life/level draining attacks of undead
creatures and various spells.

Purify Food and Drink (reversible)
Level: 1
Duration: Permanent
Range: 10'
Makes spoiled, rotten, poisonous, or otherwise contaminated food and water
pure and wholesome.

Geas
Level: 4
Duration: Special
Range: 60'
Compels target to undergo a quest of caster's choosing, save negates.  The
quest must be achievable. It can be dangerous, although the target cannot be
instructed to purposefully harm itself. Should the target resist undertaking
the quest, they will suffer increasing harm and eventually die.

Regenerate
Level: 4
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Target's severed appendages, broken bones, and ruined organs grow back in
2d4 turns or 1 round if the severed parts are present and held "in place".


Raise Dead (reversible)
Level: 5
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Restores life to a body that has been dead for no longer than two days per
level of dead guy. Dying is an ordeal, those returning have no hit points and
1 CON. Recovering a max of 1 CON per day. Until fully restored, target is weak
and may not engage in spell casting, combat, or any other strenuous activity.

Resurrection (reversible)
Level: 7
Duration: Permanent
Range: Touch
Restores a dead creature to full health and vigor. The condition or existence of
the remains is not a factor but the target can have been dead no longer than
10 years per caster level.

Sanctuary
Level: 1
Duration: 1 round per level
Range: Touch
Any opponent must make save to strike or otherwise directly attack the target.
Offensive actions by target cancels spell.

Silence 15' Radius
Level: 2
Duration: 12 turns
Range: 120'
No noise whatsoever issues from the area of effect, but noise originating from
outside the silenced area can be heard by those within it.

Tongues
Level: 2
Duration: 6 turns
Range: Self
Grants the caster the ability to read, speak and understand any normal language.

Speak with Animals
Level: 2
Duration: 6 turns
Range: Self
Caster can comprehend and communicate with ordinary and giant animals.  There
is a good chance that the animals will be friendly and eager to provide
reasonable assistance, they don't get to talk with non-animals very often.

Speak with Plants
Level: 2
Duration: 6 turns
Range: Self
Caster can communicate with plants, including normal plants, plant
creatures, funguses and the like.

Speak with Creatures
Level: 3
Duration: 6 turns
Range: Self
As |Speak with Animals|_, but caster may converse with any creature.

Speak with Dead
Level: 3
Duration: Special
Range: 1'
Corpse must answer truthfully but its knowledge is limited to what the
creature knew during life, including the languages it spoke.

Snake Charm

Duration: 1d4+2 turns
Range: 60'
Charms 1 HD of snakes per caster level which obey the caster's spoken commands.

Sticks to Snakes
Level: 2
Duration: 6 turns
Range: 60'
Transform 2d6 sticks into snakes which obey the caster's spoken commands.

Symbol
Level: 6
Duration: 1 turn per level
Range: Touch
Same as Magic-user spell of same name.

True Seeing
Level: 3
Duration: 1 round per level
Range: Self
Caster gains ability to see all things as they actually are. The target sees
through normal and magical darkness, notices secret doors, sees invisible
creatures or objects normally, sees through illusions, and sees the true form
of polymorphed, changed, or transmuted things.

Wind Walk
Level: 4
Duration: 6 turns per level
Range: Touch
Alters the substance of one target per level to a cloudlike vapor.  A wind
walker can fly with a maximum movement of 100' per caster level.

Word of Recall
Level: 3
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: Touch
Transports up to seven willing targets linked by hands instantly back to
the caster's previously sanctified sanctuary.
